Crossroads

There is Knowledge in Wisdom

One can follow many paths.

Faith relies on believing that what you have been told is true

Knowledge relies on your understanding of the world

Wisdom is accepting that belief and understanding can be part of a greater whole

To properly understand the truth, sometimes all you have to do is ask for it.

For peace of mind, we often deny truth for a comfortable lie.

One person's truth is another's convenient story. What you believe is entirely up to you.

For the closest thing to objective truth, the paths of Knowledge, Wisdom, and Faith all must be walked.
